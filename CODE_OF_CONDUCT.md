# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make this project welcoming to researchers, students, engineers, and contributors from different fields and backgrounds.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas and implementation, not people.
- Explain academic-writing suggestions with evidence or clear reasoning.
- Protect privacy and do not share sensitive manuscript content.
- Preserve the project's academic integrity principles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private or unpublished research content without permission.
- Encouraging fabricated citations, fabricated data, or unsupported claims.
- Pressuring contributors to weaken integrity safeguards.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues, comments, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.
